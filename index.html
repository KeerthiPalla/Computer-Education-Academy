Welcome to the Computer Education Academy(CEA):

Computer Education Academy(CEA) in Fairfield University is pleasent to announce the opening of the applications for 2016-2017 academic year.

CEA is a fun-filled active computer learning community for twenty 10th and 11th grade high school students. The Academy will be held from 9a.m. to 1 p.m. for 24 Saturdays during the 20016-2017 academic school year. Bus transportation and lunch will be available to all studentss at no cost to them.
